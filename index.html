Apply for a Position

Current Available Positions include:

Data Entry Coordinator

Role: Enter and maintain bottle data in the system.

Ideal For: Detail-oriented students good with spreadsheets.

Responsibilities:
- Log bottle counts from form submissions.
- Check for errors and duplicates.
- Keep leaderboard accurate.

Recycling Captain

Role: Maintain a community recycling bin and drop off collected items.

Ideal For: Middle school or underclassmen students.

Responsibilities:
- Host and manage a recycling box.
- Deliver recyclables weekly.
- Submit collection updates via form.

Neighborhood Recycling Manager

Role: Oversee local captains and communicate with leadership.

Ideal For: Juniors or seniors with leadership skills.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor Recycling Captains.
- Ensure bins are maintained.
- Act as a mentor + communication bridge.
